### About

I am a Computer Science undergraduate at NSBM Green University and the President of the NFORCE Club. I specialize in bridging the gap between scalable technical architecture and vibrant visual storytelling. 

Whether architecting robust backend services using Spring Boot and cloud-based databases, or directing the creative vision at Idis Graphix, my focus is on building impactful, user-centric experiences. I believe in breaking the frame, ditching boring templates, and designing digital products that are as visually striking as they are technically sound.
